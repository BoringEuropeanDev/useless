About The Chaos

Rediscovering the weird, wonderful web they tried to algorithm away.

What Is This?

The Useless Button is a rebellion against algorithmic predictability. In an era where every click is tracked, analyzed, and used to predict your next move, we offer something radical: true randomness.

One click transports you to one of 500+ hand-curated websites ranging from interactive art installations to forgotten GeoCities pages, from AI experiments to games that serve no purpose other than to make you smile.

The Philosophy

Remember when the internet felt like an adventure? When you'd fall down rabbit holes of personal websites, bizarre art projects, and communities existing purely for the joy of creation?

We do. And we're bringing it back.

Our Principles:

  • No Algorithms: No prediction engines. No "recommended for you." Just chaos.
  • No Tracking: Your journey through the weird web is yours alone.
  • No Monetization: These aren't sponsored links. They're digital artifacts worth preserving.
  • Maximum Serendipity: The best discoveries are the ones you weren't looking for.

Privacy First

We collect absolutely nothing on our servers. Everything is stored locally in your browser:

  • Your click count stays on your device
  • Your visit history never leaves your browser
  • Account data is stored in localStorage, not our database
  • We don't use Google Analytics or tracking pixels

Note: When you click the button, you leave our site. We can't control or monitor what those external sites do. Browse wisely.

Submit a Site
